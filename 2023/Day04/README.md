# Day 4: Scratchcards

You have a pile of scratchcards. Each card has winning numbers and numbers you have.

## Part 1

**Task:** Check which of your numbers appear in the winning numbers list.
- First match: 1 point.
- Each subsequent match: Doubles the point value.

**Goal:** How many points are they worth in total?

Example:
```text
Card 1: 41 48 83 | 83 86  6 31 17  9 48 53
```
Matches: 48, 83.
Score: 1 (for 48) * 2 (for 83) = 2 points.
