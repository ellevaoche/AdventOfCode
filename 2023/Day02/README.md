# Day 2: Cube Conundrum

You play a game with a bag of red, green, and blue cubes. The Elf shows you subsets of cubes multiple times per game.

## Part 1

**Task:** Determine which games would have been possible if the bag contained only **12 red cubes, 13 green cubes, and 14 blue cubes**.

**Goal:** Sum the IDs of the possible games.

Example:
```text
Game 1: 3 blue, 4 red; 1 red, 2 green, 6 blue; 2 green
```
Possible? Yes.
